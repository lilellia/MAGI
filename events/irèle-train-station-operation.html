The Irèle Train Station Operation was an unofficial operation performed by CIA analyst Kaskei Ari and retired 1LT/Maiden Amara Minori in the afternoon of 7:17:12 Enna 282014.096.

At 15:07, Ari intercepted an encrypted message. Despite passing through unofficial channels, the message was encrypted using one of the Clerèssian Intelligence Agency's standard encryption schemes, and decrypted to

SET CHARGE. IRÈLE T STATION. PLAT 3. 1615.

Concerned, Ari alerted his superiors, who dismissed him, stating that it was nothing more than a means of testing the agents and that having reported it was the extent of Ari's responsibility in the matter. However, Ari was unconvinced and couldn't shake the feeling that something wasn't right. When his concerns fell on deaf ears, he reached out to Minori at 15:41 to enlist her help since she was in Irèle and could quickly respond.

Having left the agency a year prior in the wake of her best friend's death in the Siel Shopping Center Incident, Minori was initially hesitant to take up the operation. However, Ari was able to convince her, and she arrived at the train station eighteen minutes before the target time.

After scanning the other platforms, she took position in the center of platform 3, where she was approached with four minutes before the target time by a middle-aged man in a gray uniform. He seemed suspicious, and when he left his bag under Minori's care before leaving for the restroom, she discovered the expected explosives under a few books. She temporarily neutralized the explosives with her magic before confronting the man in the restroom.

He was able to put up only minimal resistance before Minori led him out to the benches on the exterior of the station to wait for Ari to arrive and take her prisoner. In the meantime, she was able to identify him as Enason Lambing, a civilian accountant who had been threatened into committing the crime by unknown forces a few weeks prior.

Ari soon arrived with a bomb technician to permanently diffuse the bombs. In his follow-up interrogation and off-the-books investigation, he was not able to determine much of any information regarding the incident.
