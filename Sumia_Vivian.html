Trivia

  • Origin of spell names:
  • Her given name, Vivian, is a reference to one of Lily Ellington's friends, named Vivian. Her code name, Emerald Viper, was chosen by her namesake.
  • As another reference, her birthday (Tiri 01) being five days before Amara Minori's birthday (Tiri 06) is a reference to her namesake's birthday also being five days before Lily's. However, Lily and Vivian are the same age, though Sumia and Amara are a year apart.
