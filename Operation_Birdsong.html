Operation Birdsong was an operation undertaken by the Clerèssian Intelligence Agency on 7:17:10 Neyu 052012.311. Led by Kaskei Ari at central command and carried out by 1LT/Maidens Amara Minori and Miú Aya, it was a protection detail during the Festival of the World.

Trivia

  • Before their reunion in Siana on 7:17:12 Fis 212014.123 (443 days later), this was the last time Minori and Aya had met in person, though they did collaborate during Operation Eclipse.
